The History of Football

Football has ancient roots, with early versions played in China and Medieval Europe. The modern game was codified in England in 1863 with the formation of the Football Association.

Football Legends

These icons shaped the game with their incredible skill and achievements:

  • Pelé - Only player to win 3 World Cups
  • Diego Maradona - Famous "Hand of God" goal
  • Lionel Messi - Most Ballon d'Or wins (8)

Football Today

The modern game features:

  • VAR technology for refereeing
  • Global tournaments like the Champions League
  • Women's football growing rapidly
